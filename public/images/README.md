# Image Upload Guide

## Required Images

### 1. Logo
- **File name**: `logo.png` or `logo.svg`
- **Location**: `/public/images/logo.png`
- **Recommended size**: 512x512px or higher (square)
- **Format**: PNG with transparency or SVG
- **Usage**: Header, Footer, Browser Tab Icon

### 2. Hero Section Image
- **File name**: `hero.jpg`
- **Location**: `/public/images/hero.jpg`
- **Recommended size**: 2400x1050px (16:7 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPG or WebP
- **Usage**: Main hero image on homepage

### 3. Gallery Images
Place your gallery/project images in `/public/images/gallery/` with the following names:
- `project-1.jpg` - Modern Residence
- `project-2.jpg` - Boutique Hotel
- `project-3.jpg` - Executive Office
- `project-4.jpg` - Penthouse Suite

**Recommended sizes**: 
- Large images (1 & 4): 1200x900px (4:3 aspect ratio)
- Small images (2 & 3): 900x900px (square)
- **Format**: JPG or WebP

### 4. Favicon (Browser Tab Icon)
The logo.png will be automatically used as the favicon.

---

## How to Upload

1. **Logo**: Add your logo file as `logo.png` or `logo.svg` in `/public/images/`
2. **Hero Image**: Add your hero image as `hero.jpg` in `/public/images/`
3. **Gallery Images**: Add 4 project images named `project-1.jpg` through `project-4.jpg` in `/public/images/gallery/`

The website will automatically display these images once uploaded!
